Vancouver

City filled with endless adventures....

Geography

British Columbia's largest city ,Vancouver, is known as one of the most desirable tourist destinations and of the best places to live. Vancouver is a coastal seaport city on the western half of the Burrard Peninsula. The city is bordered on the north by coastal mountains , the south by United states , and the west by the pacific ocean and has close to 30 km waterfront paths surrounding its neighborhoods,including the seawall around stanley park.

Population

With a population over 631,486 it is one of the most ethnically and linguistically diverse cities in canada, with over 52% speaking a language other than english. With that it is also the eighth largest municipality in Canada; the Greater Vancouver metropolitan area (which includes neighbouring cities such as Burnaby, Richmond, and Surrey) is the third largest in Canada.

Interesting Fact

Vancouver was the host of the 2010 Winter Olympics and the 2010 paralympics.
